Indian Companies Hiring & Layoffs Analytics (2020–2024)

PROJECT STATUS
Ongoing / Under Development

PROJECT OVERVIEW
The Indian job market has experienced major fluctuations over recent years, with rapid hiring cycles followed by large-scale layoffs across industries. This project analyzes hiring and layoff trends of Indian companies from 2020 to 2024 to identify workforce patterns, sector stability, and emerging risks.

The goal is to generate data-driven insights useful for students, job seekers, and analysts by highlighting industry-wise growth, volatility, and future hiring trends.

OBJECTIVES
- Analyze industry-wise and company-wise hiring vs layoffs
- Identify stable and volatile sectors in the Indian job market
- Visualize net hiring trends across locations and industries
- Build predictive models (in progress) to forecast future hiring and layoffs
- Translate workforce data into career-oriented insights

DATASET & SCOPE
Time Period: 2020–2024 (5 years)
Coverage: Indian companies
Industries: IT, Pharma, FMCG, FinTech, E-commerce, Startups, EdTech

Key Metrics:
- 53M+ total hires
- 23M+ total layoffs
- 30M net hiring

Note: All values represent aggregated workforce trends, not the headcount of a single company.

TOOLS & TECHNOLOGIES
- Power BI – Interactive dashboards and visual analytics
- Python (Pandas, NumPy) – Data cleaning and preprocessing
- SQL – Structured data analysis
- DAX & Power Query – Data modeling and transformations
- Scikit-learn (Basic) – Predictive modeling (in progress)
- NLP (Planned) – Sentiment analysis from news and media

KEY FEATURES
- Interactive Power BI dashboard with filters for company, industry, and location
- Industry-wise and city-wise hiring distribution
- Net hiring analysis by company
- Actual vs predicted hiring comparison (in progress)
- KPI-driven workforce analytics

CURRENT PROGRESS

- Descriptive analytics and dashboards implemented
- Industry-wise and location-wise analysis completed
- Predictive hiring and layoff models under development
- Sentiment analysis integration in progress

FUTURE ENHANCEMENTS

- Integrate sentiment analysis from news and social media

DISCLAIMER
This project is intended for educational and analytical purposes. Insights are based on publicly available and aggregated datasets and should not be interpreted as official company employment data.
